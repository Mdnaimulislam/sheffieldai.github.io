Family Event: Exploring the Wonder of the University Fairground.

Join an exhibition from the collections of the National Fairground and Circus Archive (NFCA) at the University of Sheffield, exploring fairground decorative arts and fairground engineering. Inspired by the story of Orton ad Spooner, make your own creation and construction to the fun.
