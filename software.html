We Are a Part of the Open Source Community

We aim to make data science and artificial intelligence more accessible to interdisciplinary research by bridging gaps between data, software, and end users.

  • Do better research with our accessible, scalable, and sustainable design guided by both machine learning experts and end users.
  • Enable and accelerate artificial intelligence research in various applications.
  • Provide tutorials for end-users to extend out software.
